Government Structure

AETHERIA is organized as a layered republic: a constitutional core sets direction, a strategic cabinet governs policy, operational offices deliver cross-cutting services, service branches connect citizens to the state, and co-equal branches provide legislative and judicial checks. Reporting lines flow downward, while peer and checks-and-balances lines run across layers.

Layered Republic

The government is not a mirror of legacy territorial ministries. Every role is justified by our actual medium: code, data, and digital identity. The five layers preserve separation of powers while keeping authority aligned to outcomes instead of hierarchy.

Constitutional Core

President, Vice President, and Constitutional Council. Sets strategy, interprets charter, oversees appointments, and holds veto over charter amendments.

Strategic Cabinet

Six active secretaries reporting to the President: External Affairs, Digital Economy & Commerce, Civic Identity & Registry, Public Affairs & Culture, Technology, Science & Innovation, and Infrastructure & Human Services. (Justice & Constitutional Compliance is retired; judicial functions now rest with the Constitutional Court.)

Operational Offices

Four specialized operational arms execute cross-cutting functions: Digital Operations, Data Governance, Open Records, and Situational Awareness. Together they provide platform reliability, data ethics, transparency, and strategic foresight as operational arms with specialized mandates.

Service Branches

Digital Service Corps and Electoral Commission. They deliver civic and operational services under presidential coordination.

Checks & Balances

Assembly initiates and passes legislation, approves budgets, confirms appointments, and oversees elections. The Constitutional Court issues binding rulings on charter disputes and constitutional questions.

Interface Layer

Civic-facing pages, protocols, diplomacy, and transparency outputs that present the nation internally and externally.

Governance Principles

Digital-native design over territorial inheritance. Merit and performance govern advancement. Information is compartmentalized on need-to-know. Transparency is the default. Community voice is embedded in operations. Data is treated as a national asset. Foundational rules are protected by long-term institutional roles.
