What Citizenship Gives You

Self-Sovereign Identity

A cryptographic citizen ID you control. No one can revoke it without your consent.

Public Network Access

Connect to decentralized services, nodes, and community channels from anywhere.

Governance Rights

Vote on standards, budgets, and policy. Every citizen's voice counts on equal footing.

Open Services

Use the Citizen Hub, datasets, transparency records, and digital service programs without gatekeeping.

Interoperable Participation

Contribute across governance, engineering, documentation, outreach, security, and node operation paths.

Long-Term Access

Maintain continuity across program changes with portable identity and preserved participation history.
