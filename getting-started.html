Glossary of Digital Nation Terms

Citizen

A verified participant with access to core services and governance rights.

Charter

The foundational governance document defining rights, roles, and decision procedures.

Assembly

The deliberative body for legislation, budget approval, and policy direction.

Protocol

Published technical and procedural standards for interoperability and transparency.

Sovereignty

The principle that individuals control their digital identity and participation without external coercion.

Citizen Hub

A central gateway to services, tools, records, and support for all citizens.
