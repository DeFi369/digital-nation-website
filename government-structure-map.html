Constitutional Core

President, Vice President, Constitutional Council. Sets strategy, interprets charter, oversees appointments.

Strategic Cabinet

Seven secretaries: External Affairs, Digital Economy, Civic Identity, Public Affairs, Technology & Innovation, Infrastructure & Human Services, Justice & Constitutional Compliance.

Operational Offices

Digital Operations, Data Governance, Open Records, and Situational Awareness.

Service Branches

Digital Service Corps and Electoral Commission deliver civic and operational services under presidential coordination.

Checks & Balances

Assembly confirms appointments, approves amendments, and passes resolutions. Charter Tribunal issues binding rulings on disputes and constitutional questions.

Interface Layer

Civic-facing pages, protocols, diplomacy, and transparency outputs that present the nation internally and externally.
