Acceptance

By participating in AETHERIA services, governance, or credential programs, you accept these terms and the Charter. If you do not agree, do not use the services or request citizenship.

Citizenship

Citizenship is granted based on contribution and verification, not purchase. It may be revoked through documented protocol procedures. Citizens are expected to follow ratified governance decisions and respect the rights of others.

Acceptable Use

Do not abuse services, interfere with governance, attempt credential fraud, or violate the privacy of other citizens. Violations may result in protocol-level restrictions or revocation review.

Services & Availability

AETHERIA services are provided on an as-is basis without uptime guarantees beyond documented SLAs. The Principality may update, suspend, or deprecate services through governance processes.

Open Protocol

Published standards and protocols may be adopted by others. Contributions submitted to AETHERIA may be included in public specifications unless explicitly marked otherwise.

Limitation of Liability

AETHERIA is an experimental digital polity. Participation involves technical and governance risk. To the maximum extent allowed by applicable law, AETHERIA is not liable for loss of keys, service disruption, or indirect damages arising from participation.

Changes

Terms may be amended through the Civic Assembly. Material changes are announced through public governance channels before taking effect.
