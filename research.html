AI Research

Digital Nation AI research is directed toward sovereign, verifiable intelligence — systems that reason over public records, protocol state, and citizen data without outsourcing trust. The current focus is agent-world integration and verifiable governance substrates, with emphasis on reproducible research and open attestation.

WorldHermes is the principal sovereign AI research project under this direction: github.com/defi369/WorldHermes. It pairs LeWorldModel with the Hermes agent runtime to ground tool use in world-state understanding rather than opaque model memory, aligning with Digital Nation’s broader commitment to transparency and auditability.

The underlying research substrate is the AEP/dynAEP/GAP protocol stack: Protocol for governance structure, Protocol Status for live attestation, dynAEP for temporal/runtime event governance, and GAP for schema-validated policy language. This stack is intended not only as operational infrastructure, but as a publishable research artifact with provable invariants.
