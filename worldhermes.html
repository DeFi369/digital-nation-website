WorldHermes — AI Research

WorldHermes is the Digital Nation’s sovereign AI research programme. It integrates the LeWorldModel world-model substrate with Hermes Agent so that executive decisions are grounded in a compact, auditable world-state model rather than pure prompt-guessing.

Why This Matters

Sovereign grounding

A native world model lets the Digital Nation maintain its own situational understanding instead of outsourcing cognition to external APIs.

Deterministic audit trail

World state transitions can be hashed, checkpointed, and reviewed under the same evidence-ledger guarantees as the rest of the AEP stack.

Research continuity

WorldHermes preserves long-horizon reasoning across sessions, which supports the Digital Nation’s long-term governance and infrastructure planning.
