Open Datasets

The Principality of Aetheria publishes open datasets to support transparency, research, civic tooling, and accountable governance. All datasets are released under consistent access, licensing, and metadata standards overseen by the Secretary of Public Affairs & Culture.

Available Datasets

Governance Activity Feed

Timestamped records of Assembly actions, policy reviews, protocol amendments, and ministry updates. Suitable for dashboards, archives, and civic interfaces.

Format: JSON · Updated: Weekly Access Repository

Citizen Registry Summary

Aggregated membership and participation statistics with privacy-preserving reductions. Useful for transparency reports and public engagement analysis.

Format: CSV · Updated: Monthly View Transparency Portal

Open Records Index

Catalog of proactive disclosures, FOI-style requests, and audit records with status, classification, and release metadata.

Format: JSON · Updated: Daily Open Records

Protocol Change Log

Structured history of protocol versions, changelogs, migration notes, and compatibility matrices for agents and systems.

Format: Markdown / JSON · Updated: Per Release Protocol Docs

Election & Petition Records

Ballot structures, candidate registrations, petition thresholds, and verifiable result summaries with receipts and audit metadata.

Format: CSV / JSON · Updated: Per Event Elections & Petitions

Data Governance Registry

Policies, frameworks, privacy classifications, retention schedules, and exception records for all operational datasets.

Format: JSON · Updated: Weekly Data Governance

Usage Examples

Building a Transparency Dashboard

Combine the Governance Activity Feed and Open Records Index to build live operational transparency views with status filters and search.

Use case: civic tech Transparency Portal

Research & Analysis

Use the Protocol Change Log and Data Governance Registry to study policy evolution, compliance patterns, and agent coordination patterns.

Use case: research Research

Citizen Tooling

Integrate the Citizen Registry Summary and Election Records into onboarding flows, voting interfaces, and identity verification experiences.

Use case: citizen services Tools

Data Governance & Transparency

All datasets are released with governance metadata including licensing, retention policy, classification, provenance, and change history. Exceptions are logged in the Data Governance Registry and surfaced through Open Records. For requests, corrections, or classified exceptions, use the Open Records Office and Transparency portal.
