Infrastructure & Human Services — Health

Health is foundational to national productivity. Under the Secretary of Infrastructure & Human Services, this ministry coordinates privacy-preserving digital health services, wellness programmes, and resilient public-health infrastructure within Aetheria's v3.0 civic substrate.

Ministerial Mandate

The Health portfolio delivers identity-anchored, privacy-preserving health services to all citizens — from secure telehealth access and emergency response readiness to accessible wellness tooling and civic health interoperability. All services operate on auditable consent controls and verifiable provider identities within the v3.0 governance framework.
