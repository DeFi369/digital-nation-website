What Citizenship Gives You

Self-Sovereign Identity

A cryptographic citizen ID you control. No one can revoke it without your consent.

Public Network Access

Connect to decentralized services, nodes, and community channels from anywhere.

Governance Rights

Vote on standards, budgets, and policy. Every citizen's voice counts on equal footing.

Getting Started

1. Read the framework

Review the current charter, operational standards, and published decision history before applying.

2. Choose a contribution path

Engineering, governance, documentation, outreach, security, or node operation. You decide where you add the most value.

3. Request access

Submit a short introduction and intended contribution area. The verification process is transparent and usually completes within a few days.
