Digital Asset Framework

Issuance Rules

Digital assets are issued against documented reserves with on-protocol attestation. Issuance parameters are fixed at creation and immutable thereafter.

Custody & Settlement

Non-custodial by default. Settlement is deterministic and final, with verifiable state transitions and audit-ready event logs.

Compliance Layers

Asset classes are tagged with compliance metadata: jurisdiction restrictions, transfer limits, and regulatory reporting hooks.

Fiscal Reserves

Operating Reserve

Liquidity reserve maintained for program continuity and operational expenses. Target coverage ratios are published in quarterly reports.

Strategic Reserve

Long-duration holdings for systemic stability and protocol backstop. Deployment requires dual approval from Treasury and Assembly.

Grant Reserve

Funds earmarked for public-good grants, bounties, and civic prizes. Allocation follows transparent scoring and verification pipelines.
