Ambassadors

Ambassadors are citizens who help expand Aetheria’s reach across events, communities, protocols, and projects. They are the human face of the Digital Nation.

About the Program

The Ambassador Program supports active citizens who want to represent Aetheria externally while building stronger internal culture. Ambassadors help onboard new citizens, speak at public events, document processes, and keep local communities informed about protocol updates and opportunities.

Start by engaging with the citizen community and reviewing the onboarding path. When you are ready, submit your interest through Join and note Ambassador Program as your preferred track.
