Constitutional Court

The Constitutional Court is the co-equal judicial branch of AETHERIA. It serves as the final arbiter of charter disputes, constitutional questions, and judicial review. It may strike actions that violate the charter, and its rulings are binding on all departments. Members are appointed by the Chair of the Constitutional Council.
