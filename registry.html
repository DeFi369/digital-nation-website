Verification Tiers

Tier 1 — Verified

Minimum verified citizen status after completed onboarding and public-record verification.

Tier 2 — Active

Ongoing governance participation, service usage, and documented contribution across protocol channels.

Tier 3 — Elevated

Extended trust level for ambassadors, node operators, and representatives with verified operational responsibilities.

Public Record Notices

Registry entries may display identity anchors, tier history, and public participation metadata. No private credentials or key material are published. For context on how records are used for credentials, see Passport and Citizenship. To engage with governance using verified status, visit Engage.
