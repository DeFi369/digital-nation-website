Assembly of the Digital Nation

The Assembly is the co-equal legislative branch of AETHERIA. It initiates legislation, approves budgets, confirms appointments, passes resolutions, and oversees elections. The Chief Electoral Officer reports to the Assembly. Verified citizens track bills through introduction, committee referral, debate, and vote in real time.
