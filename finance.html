Budget Transparency

Published Budgets

All approved budgets are published with line-item visibility. Citizens can inspect allocations by ministry, program, and reporting period.

Reconciliation Reports

Quarterly reconciliation compares planned versus actual spending. Variance explanations are required for material deviations.

Open Accounting Standards

Financial statements follow deterministic, verifiable accounting rules. Independent review is enabled through published attestation artifacts.

Economic Governance

Policy Committee

A cross-functional body reviews fiscal proposals before Assembly voting. Membership rotates by region and sector to prevent capture.

Spending Thresholds

Programs above defined thresholds require additional review layers and citizen comment windows before disbursement approval.

Anti-Corruption Controls

Automated compliance checks, whistleblower channels, and transparent audit trails reduce opportunities for misuse of public funds.
