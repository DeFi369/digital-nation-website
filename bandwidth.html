National Broadband & Digital Equity

Universal connectivity access through deployment programmes, access standards, coverage metrics, and digital equity policy aligned with Infrastructure, Technology, and Education under the Secretary of Infrastructure & Human Services.

Ministerial Mandate

The Broadband portfolio delivers universal connectivity as a civic right — through high-capacity fiber backbone, rural last-mile mesh, community Wi-Fi, and submarine cable redundancy. All programmes enforce affordable access tariffs, digital equity ratings, and device distribution with setup support, ensuring connectivity costs never block civic participation.
